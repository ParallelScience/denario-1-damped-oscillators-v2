The failure to analyze the "High" damping regime (as noted in the results table) is directly attributable to the dataset description. The "Contents" section specifies that the `damping_ratio` for all oscillators is less than 1 (underdamped), but it does not guarantee a uniform distribution across the range $[0, 1)$. The absence of high-damping oscillators in the provided 20-oscillator sample prevents the research plan from completing the intended population-wide sensitivity mapping.
