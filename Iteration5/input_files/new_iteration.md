- Justification: The current research has identified a "valley of degeneracy" and established the sensitivity of the energy manifold, but the critique correctly identifies that the current results are confounded by the signal-to-noise ratio (SNR) decay. The proposed changes—specifically normalizing residuals by instantaneous energy and using SVD to quantify the condition number of the sensitivity matrix—are not merely "technical layers"; they are fundamental to establishing the scientific validity of the "ill-posedness" claim. By distinguishing between model-induced divergence and noise-induced divergence, the paper moves from a descriptive observation to a rigorous, publishable analysis of parameter identifiability limits. The ROI is high because these changes leverage existing data and code structures while significantly increasing the "Reviewer Confidence" by addressing the statistical robustness of the conclusions.
